Menu of the day

Salad

Our salad of the day is the Cobb Salad, which has it all for everyone and for each taste. This salad is full of fresh flavors and textures, protein, fiber, vitamins and minerals. It is dairy-free, egg-free and gluten-free and you don't have to worry about the calories. The main components of our recipe are romaine, coconut bacon, chickpeas, corn, tomatoes, radish, avocado, red onion and our famous vegan ranch dressing. Don't hesitate and choose our unique salad!

And what makes our soup unique are our secret seasonings. Taste it right now!
